# Discord

The official Pulsate Discord is the place to get the latest news and interact with other community members.

<Cards>
    <Card title="Join the Pulsate Discord ↗" href="https://link.pulsate.dev/discord" />
</Cards>

<Callout type={"warning"}>
    To prevent vandalism, users whose accounts have not been created for more than one week cannot participate.
</Callout>

## Rules

Community members must follow Rust's [Code of Conduct](https://www.rust-lang.org/policies/code-of-conduct), [Discord Community Guidelines](https://discord.com/guidelines).

## Notification settings

To receive announcements and release information about Pulsate, you need to obtain an exclusive role.

<Tabs items={["For first-time participants", "For those already participating"]}>
    <Tabs.Tab>
        Choose your preferred option on the notification settings screen that appears when you join Discord.

        ![Notification settings screen](/image/community/discord/discord-notifications-panel-en.jpg)
    </Tabs.Tab>
    <Tabs.Tab>
        You can change it in the `Customise` tab under `#Channels & Roles`.

        - `Announcement`: receive all announcements about Pulsate.
        - `Release`: receive information about Pulsate releases.

        ![Notification settings screen](/image/community/discord/discord-channels-and-roles.jpg)
    </Tabs.Tab>
</Tabs>

## How to report to a moderator

In the unlikely event that you find a violation of the Code of Conduct or Discord Community Guidelines, please report it to a moderator.

<Tabs items={["Report by bot", "Report by email", "Mentions (spam only)"]}>
    <Tabs.Tab>
        <Callout type={"warning"}>
            This function is only available for reporting messages. For violations in text channels, forum channels, etc., please use **Report by email**.
        </Callout>

        1. Right-click (long-press for mobile version) on the message.
        2. Select `Apps` and then `Report Message`.
        3. Enter a reason.
    </Tabs.Tab>
    <Tabs.Tab>
        Send your report to [`moderator@pulsate.dev`](mailto:moderator@pulsate.dev). Please note that no replies will be sent from this email address.
    </Tabs.Tab>
    <Tabs.Tab>
        <Callout type={"warning"}>
            Only spam and other unwanted activities can be reported. Other reports will not be dealt with.
        </Callout>

        Report it with `@mod` and a mentions.
    </Tabs.Tab>
</Tabs>
